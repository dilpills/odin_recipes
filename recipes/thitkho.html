How to make Thit Kho

What is Thit Kho?

This is a classic Vietnamese dish made by braising pork belly & in coconut soda.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Eggs
  • Green Onion
  • Fish Sauce
  • Coconut Cream Soda

Steps

  1. Cut pork belly into 2"x2" cubes.
  2. Add 1 TBSP sugar to pot.
  3. Caramelize the sugar.
  4. Add pork and stir.
  5. Add 2 cans coconut soda and simmer.
  6. Cook until pork is tender.
  7. Reduce until desired consistency.
  8. Season with fish sauce.
  9. Garnish with green onion.
